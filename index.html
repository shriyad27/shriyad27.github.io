MEET CECE

An intelligent, quick, and curious virtual assistant designed to help all students with their application processes. This chatbot is very informational and easy to use! By asking the chatbot a question, you can get relevant information right away without having to surf the internet. CeCe is designed to learn information as it is used. Therefore, if the chatbot does not know the answer to your question at the moment, we will do our best to supply it with information it needs to answer the question in the future. Through the use of the chatbot, we hope to make the process easier to understand and more feasible to attain information.

To use the chatbot, click the chat image in the space below. The service is still under development, so feel free to leave any suggestions regarding useability, visual appeal, or functionality! Thanks in advance.
